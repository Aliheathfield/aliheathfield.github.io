The Book

Not Done Yet: Discovering What's Possible After 60

After her husband Brian died just two days after her 65th birthday, Ali Heathfield faced the question that arrives for so many of us in later life: let life happen, or live with intention. She chose intention.

That decision led her to Portugal, to a new language, a new community, and ultimately to the Living Life Well model — a practical map for anyone who suspects their best chapters are still ahead.

This book helps you shape your own encore — ageing with intention, not by default.

Whether you are navigating significant change, managing your health, or simply wanting to live more fully, Not Done Yet gives you the framework, the questions, and the encouragement to move forward.
