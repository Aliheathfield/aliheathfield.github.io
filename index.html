The Book

Not Done Yet: Life After 60 — Ten Themes To Create What's Possible.

Born from personal experience and grounded in positive psychology, Not Done Yet is a practical map for anyone who suspects their best chapters are still ahead.

This book helps you shape your own encore — ageing with intention, not by default.

Whether you are navigating significant change, managing your health, or simply wanting to live more fully, Not Done Yet gives you the framework, the questions, and the encouragement to move forward. It is honest about the hard parts — and deeply practical about what to do with them.
