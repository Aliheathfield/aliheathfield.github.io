Privacy & Legal

Plain English. No jargon. Everything you need to know about how this site works and how your information is handled.

Last updated: April 2025

Who runs this site

This website is operated by Ali Heathfield, a life coach and author based in Portugal. Ali works with individuals navigating life beyond 60 through coaching, online courses, and the tools and resources on this site.

For any questions about this site or its content, please use the contact details at the bottom of this page.

What data this site collects

This website currently collects no personal data from visitors. There are no contact forms, no email sign-up fields, no user accounts, and no login system on this site.

The free tools available to download from the Resources page (such as the Life Check-In) store any information you enter locally on your own device, using your browser's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to Ali or anyone else, and is entirely under your own control. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

In short: Ali does not have access to anything you write in the tools. Your reflections are your own.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not currently use cookies or any analytics tracking. No data about your visit — pages viewed, time spent, location, or device — is collected or stored.

This may change in the future. If analytics are added, this page will be updated and, where required by law, you will be asked for your consent before any tracking takes place. Any analytics tool used will be chosen with privacy in mind.

Third-party links

This site may contain links to external websites, including Substack, Amazon, and other platforms. Once you leave this site, Ali has no control over and takes no responsibility for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reading their privacy policies before sharing any personal information with them.

Your rights under GDPR

Because Ali is based in Portugal and this site is accessible to readers in the EU and UK, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is relevant. Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is held about you
  • Request that any personal data be corrected or deleted
  • Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

As this site currently collects no personal data, most of these rights have no practical application at present. Should this change, this page will be updated accordingly. If you have any concerns, please get in touch.
