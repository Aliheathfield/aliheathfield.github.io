The Life Check-In

Not sure where to begin, or simply curious about how things stand right now? The Life Check-In is a guided reflection across the ten themes of the Living Life Well model. It takes around 20 minutes and gives you a clear, honest picture of where you are — and where you might want to focus.

No right or wrong answers. No scoring against someone else. Just a quiet, structured moment with yourself.

How It Works

Three steps to get started.

1

Download the file

Click the link above. The file will download to your device — it is a single HTML file, around 100KB.

2

Open it in your browser

Double-click the downloaded file. It will open in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge — whichever you use.

3

Work through it at your own pace

Take your time. Your answers are saved automatically to your own device. You can return to it whenever you like.

A note on privacy

The Life Check-In stores everything locally on your own device using your browser's built-in storage. Nothing is sent anywhere, nothing is tracked, and nothing leaves your computer. Ali does not have access to your answers. Your data is entirely your own.
