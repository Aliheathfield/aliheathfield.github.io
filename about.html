About

Ali Heathfield

Ali in Portugal

I listen. I sit with you, work to understand what is really going on, and then walk alongside you using the map I developed for Living Life Well.

I weave in my knowledge of positive psychology, coaching and life wisdom until you have found what works best for you at this particular stage of life.

I hold an MSc in Positive Psychology with Distinction from Buckinghamshire New University and bring decades of coaching experience working with individuals through transition and growth.

The Approach

Working beneath the surface.

Trained deeply in how people think, communicate and change, I hear what is beneath the surface as much as what is said — working with the whole person, not just the presenting question.

Real change often lives beneath what we say. I approach that deeper level of the work with care, curiosity and great respect for the person doing it.

My approach is warm, practical and grounded in lived experience. What you see is what you get.

How I Work

Working with the whole person.

I work with individuals navigating life beyond 60 — whether facing significant change, managing health challenges, or simply wanting to live more fully and intentionally.

  • One-to-one coaching Personal, in-depth work using the Living Life Well model — at your own pace.
  • Online courses Structured learning grounded in positive psychology and practical application.
  • Interactive toolkits Digital tools that accompany the work — free and paid options available.
  • Retreats Immersive experiences grounded in the Living Life Well model.

The Person

Born in the UK. Living in Portugal.

I have two children, three grandchildren, a sister, nieces and nephews, and a firm belief that the best chapters need not be behind us. Taking my own advice, I continue to learn Portuguese.

When I am not working, you will find me at the piano, walking, painting, deep in an audiobook, or spending time with my cats. My cats, meanwhile, continue to teach me who is boss.

Ali's book, Not Done Yet, publishes 1st June 2026.
